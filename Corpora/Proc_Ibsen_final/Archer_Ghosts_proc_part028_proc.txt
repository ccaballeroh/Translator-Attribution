[Springs up in despair, entwines her hands in her hair and shrieks.] I cannot bear it! [Whispers, as though petrified]; I cannot bear it! Never! [Suddenly.] Where has he got them? [Fumbles hastily in his breast.] Here! [Shrinks back a few steps and screams:] No! No; no!--Yes!--No; no! [She stands a few steps away from him with her hands twisted in her hair, and stares at him in speechless horror.] OSWALD. [Sits motionless as before and says.] The sun.--The sun.
